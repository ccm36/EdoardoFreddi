About Us

Edoardo Freddi USA is a specialized importing agency that directly partners with carefully selected Italian wineries, representing them across the United States.

We are wine enthusiasts who curate individual wine strategies for different clients, assisting them from marketing campaigns to the shelf rotation in store. From the well-known vineyard estates of Piemonte to the Sicilian coasts, the Edoardo Freddi USA portfolio encompasses some of Italy’s finest and most historic wine making estates.

Our mission is to become reliable wine consultants and partners for retailers providing quality Italian wine at a competitive price. Edoardo Freddi USA strives in creating mutually profitable partnerships between Italy and United States.

Our Business Model

Edoardo Freddi USA partners with one of the biggest importing companies to bring products in through New Jersey (NJ). We work using a Direct Importing Method, along with the possibility to warehouse when needed.

Our main competitive advantage derives in the ability act as a commercial force on behalf of our suppliers, making us their exclusive representative in USA. By managing both the commercial activity and the import in the US we are able to offer the best prices on the market.

Our Team

Edoardo Freddi

CEO

Edoardo has his Bachelor’s Degree in Economics and Management and his Master’s in Food and Beverage Management from the SDA Bocconi in Milan. After gaining almost a decade of experience in international wine sales, he decided to expand to the United States, bringing his experience and proven business model.

Filippo Volodin

Director

Filippo comes from a strong background of international sales. He has worked alongside his father importing Italian food and beverage products to the largest retail chains around the world. His previous experiences in Italy, China, Russia, and the USA allowed him to develop the skills needed to understand the needs and wants of the wine market in the US.

John Moore

National Chain & Account Manager

John brings over 33 years of experience in the wine industry starting in 1986. He has worked for distributors, importers, and suppliers throughout the US, and has had enormous success building strong partnerships with National Chain Accounts throughout the country. John has been a top contributor in the wine industry for years, and will continue to have success with Edoardo Freddi USA.
